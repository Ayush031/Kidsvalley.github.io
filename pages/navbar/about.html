Who we are?

Kids Valley is the flagship brand. We are The Nation's leading Preschool Education provider.

What do we do?

Our Nursery schools are concerned with the early development of children and introducing them to structured learning through a systematic and innovative way.

Where we are ?

As on date, we have a nationwide reach with a Chain of 1100+ Pre Schools spread across the length and breadth of the country.

What's next ?

We've expanded into the (10+2) formal schooling segment with a network of 100+ Academic Heights Public Schools (AHPS) across the country.

What's new ?

New feathers in the cap are Prismart Classes, Speak-O-Kit, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Robotime and Pri-Tab.
