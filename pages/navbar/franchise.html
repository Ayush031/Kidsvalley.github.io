About Kids Valley play school franchise

About Kids Valley play school franchise Kids Valley is the fastest growing play school chain & top education franchise in India. We started operating back in 2004 & in a span of 15 years have taught more than 5 lakh young kids. Kids Valley has a network of 1200+ schools across India which operate in 400+ cities. We have won more than 50 different awards in education segments including innovation & curriculum as a mainstream category. It has also been awarded as ‘Preschool Franchisor of the year’ consecutively for two years ranging from 2017 – 2019. Kids Valley has gone global now & is coming up with schools in Nepal & Bangladesh. The topmost benefit of applying for a leading and best preschool franchise is the brand goodwill and proven business model which it follows. Kids Valley has been a preferred choice for thousands of business aspirants who always wanted to take up kids’ school franchise as a venture. Kids Valley Play School offers a profitable option of starting a nursery school franchise. This opportunity ranges from a venturist who is young in age to the oldest person willing to start a preschool. All that is required to start your own play school is minimum 2000 sqft. area & an investment of Rs. 12+ lakh.
